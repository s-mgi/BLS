The Industry Challenge

Your sales process is
leaking money.

Today's new home sales run across a patchwork of disconnected tools — spreadsheets, email chains, paper worksheets, ERPs that don't talk to each other. Every handoff is a chance for something to break.

  • Duplicate data entry across multiple platforms

    Every deal gets typed twice — at least.

  • Sales and ERP systems with no real-time sync

    Your ERP is always one step behind what actually happened.

  • Manual worksheets and approvals slow every deal down

    Paper forms that get lost, scanned wrong, or re-entered badly.

  • Delayed reporting means decisions come too late

    By the time the report lands, the window has already closed.

  • Administrative bottlenecks and compounding errors

    The more hands touch the data, the more ways it can go wrong.
